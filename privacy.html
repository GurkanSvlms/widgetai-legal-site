WidgetAI

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Overview

WidgetAI is a personal productivity, habits, nutrition, and widget app. The app is designed to keep your personal data on your device whenever possible.

Data You Create

The app may store tasks, habits, routines, nutrition logs, water logs, sleep entries, profile settings, app preferences, and widget settings locally on your device.

Permissions

WidgetAI may ask for optional access to notifications, calendar, reminders, HealthKit, location, camera, and photo library. These permissions are used only for the features you choose to use.

AI Features

If you use AI chat, AI suggestions, or meal photo scanning, the text, app context, or selected image required for that request may be sent to the AI service provider to generate a response. Do not submit sensitive information you do not want processed by an AI service.

Health and Nutrition

Nutrition and AI meal scan results are estimates for personal tracking only. They are not medical advice. Health data access is optional and can be managed in iOS Settings.

Subscriptions

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. WidgetAI does not receive or store your full payment card details.

Data Export and Deletion

You can export or remove app data using in-app tools where available, or by deleting the app from your device. Some Apple-managed purchase records may remain available through your Apple ID.
